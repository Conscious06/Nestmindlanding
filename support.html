Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create a family?

Creating a family in NestMind takes about 30 seconds:

  1. Open the App and sign in (or create an account if you haven't already).
  2. On the Home screen, tap Create a Family.
  3. Give your family a name — something like "The Johnsons" or just "Home" works great.
  4. Tap Create. You're now the family admin.

As the family creator, you can invite other members, add child profiles, and manage family settings. You can also join an existing family if someone has already invited you — look for the invitation in your notifications.

How do I invite family members?

To invite someone to your family group:

  1. Go to the Family tab at the bottom of the screen.
  2. Tap Invite Member.
  3. Enter their email address or share the invite link via iMessage, WhatsApp, or any other app.

When they receive the invite and open the App, they'll be prompted to join your family. If they don't have NestMind yet, the link will take them to the App Store first.

Note: The free plan supports up to 3 family members. Upgrade to NestMind Premium to add more.

Can I delete my account and all my data?

Yes. You have full control over your data and can delete your account at any time:

  1. Open the App and go to Settings (tap your avatar in the top-right corner).
  2. Scroll to the bottom and tap Account.
  3. Tap Delete Account and confirm.

Deleting your account permanently removes your profile, login credentials, and all personal data from our systems within 30 days. Content you created within a shared family group (tasks, events) will remain visible to other members but will be marked as belonging to a removed user.

If you are the only member of your family group, deleting your account will also delete the entire family and all its content. If you'd prefer to request deletion by email, contact us at nestmindsupport@gmail.com.

How do notifications work?

NestMind uses Apple Push Notifications (via Firebase Cloud Messaging) to keep your family in sync. You'll receive notifications for:

  • New task assigned to you — when someone creates a task and assigns it to you.
  • Task due soon — a reminder before your task's deadline.
  • Task completed — when a task you created gets marked done.
  • Task declined or renegotiated — when an assigned member can't complete a task as requested.
  • Routine reminders — at the time you've scheduled a routine to start.

You can adjust which notifications you receive from within the App under Settings › Notifications, or manage them at the system level in your iPhone's Settings › Notifications › NestMind.

NestMind never sends marketing push notifications. All notifications are directly related to activity within your family group.
